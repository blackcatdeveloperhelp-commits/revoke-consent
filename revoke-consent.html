About data consent in Who?

Who? may display personalized ads and collect limited gameplay data to improve your experience. This can include language preferences, category choices, and (where permitted) advertising identifiers for analytics and ad personalization.

You can revoke your consent at any time if you no longer wish for your data to be used for these purposes.

What happens when you revoke consent

  • Personalized ads will be disabled (you will see generic ads only).
  • Analytics used to personalize content and ads will be limited.
  • Previously collected ad identifiers will no longer be used for personalization.
  • You can keep playing Who? normally, just with reduced personalization.

How to revoke your consent

You can manage or revoke your privacy and advertising choices directly inside the Who? app:

  1. Open the Who? app on your device;
  2. Go to Profile → Delete My Account;
  3. On that screen you will find:
    • Information about account deletion;
    • A button to Delete My Account Permanently;
    • And a button to Revoke Consent (stop using personal data for ads and analytics).
Go to Delete My Account Scroll to Revoke Consent section

When you tap Revoke Consent inside the app, your ad permissions are reset. The consent screen may appear again on the next app open so you can choose your preferences.

Revoke consent (inside the app)

This website is informational. To actually revoke consent, open the Who? app and use the button Revoke Consent available under Profile → Delete My Account. That option triggers the consent form to be shown again so you can change your choice.

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you will see the GDPR consent form. In the United States, you may see a state privacy prompt (e.g., California CCPA/CPRA). In other regions, the app will continue without extra prompts.

Need to delete your account and data?

Account deletion is permanent and removes your progress, scores, purchases, preferences, and gameplay history from our servers.
